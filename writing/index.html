How I Write
🌑
Atmospheric dread
The horror is in what's not said. The silence between sentences. The detail that's just slightly wrong.
🧍
Unreliable narrators
The story you're reading might not be the story that happened. The reader figures it out before the narrator does.
🌏
Bangladeshi roots
Folklore, monsoons, rivers, rickshaws, load-shedding at midnight. The setting is never neutral.
😏
Dark humour
Even horror needs a Chandler Bing moment. Wit is a coping mechanism — in fiction and in life.
The scariest things are the ones that make perfect sense in the morning — and still scare you anyway.
— on why I write horror, probably
